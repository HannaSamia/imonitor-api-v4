## Summary

Replace all remaining `process.env` direct accesses in main-thread NestJS services
with `ConfigService.get()`, and register missing env vars in `env.validation.ts`.
This eliminates the dependency on calling `process.env` outside of bootstrap/worker
contexts, ensuring all config is validated at startup via Joi.

## Context

CLAUDE.md requires all main-thread services to use `ConfigService.get()` instead of
`process.env`. Phase 3.8 identified this as pre-work but it was deferred. The
`DB_DATA_NAME` in `dashboard.gateway.ts` was fixed in `v0.4.1`; four services remain.
Worker scripts (`src/scripts/worker/`) are exempt — NestJS DI is unavailable in
`worker_threads`/`child_process`. `data-source.ts`, `main.ts` pre-bootstrap reads,
`logger.service.ts` module-level constants, and `NODE_ENV` checks in
`scheduler.service.ts` / `global-exception.filter.ts` are also exempt.
